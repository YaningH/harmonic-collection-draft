Accept Your Emotions

Missing him doesn’t mean you should go back. It just means you’re human, with real emotions that take time to heal.

Start with acknowledging that it’s okay to feel this way. Heartbreak is a natural response to losing someone who mattered to you.

Rather than pushing the sadness away or feeling ashamed for still caring, give yourself permission to fully experience those emotions.

Let it out

Cry if you need to

Take some time to sit with your thoughts in a quiet space.

Bottling everything up only prolongs the pain, while letting it out helps you process what you're going through. You might want him back, but it’s important to remind yourself why things ended and why you deserve a connection that truly fulfills you.

Acceptance doesn’t mean you’ll immediately stop feeling sad. It just means you’ll stop fighting those feelings. Over time, they’ll lose their intensity, and you’ll find yourself making space for new experiences.
