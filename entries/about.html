Letting go of someone isn't easy. The memories linger like echoes, replaying in the quiet moments when you least expect them. It’s like shedding a layer of yourself, realizing that person took a part of your time, your thoughts, maybe even your heart. But getting over them isn’t about erasing the memories. It’s accepting that not every connection is meant to last, and sometimes, moving on is an act of self-love. You heal by finding yourself again, piece by piece, in the spaces they once occupied.
